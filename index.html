How to find a cheap flight?

1.Book in advance

It's best to book your tickets at least 3 months in advance; after that, prices tend to go up the closer you get to your departure date. Book 3–5 months in advance for short-haul flights, 5–6 months for medium-haul flights, and 8 months for long-haul flights.

2.Keep your dates flexible

Prices vary from day to day and depend on the season. The cheapest flights can be found outside the school holidays and by avoiding weekends. Take off mid-week and avoid returning home on a Sunday if possible.

3.Get instant price alerts

Schedule price alerts for your favourite destinations and receive an email notification when Liligo detects a price change. This is the best way to stay up-to-date with changing flight prices and know when the best time to book really is.
